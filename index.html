Experience and Research

As an undergraduate student I have explored several different avenues of electrical engineering. I contributed to the development of autonomous navigation stack of the rover, programming arduino to work with ROS (Robot Operating System) in Raspberry Pi companion computer, feedback controller design, etc. Used ROS to implement SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping), sensor fusion (ekf filter), Path planning, etc. In the robotics are, I am also working in ANTS Aerial Systems Ltd., where they work with drones.
